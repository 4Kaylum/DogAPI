The Dog API

Submit a Dog

This page is a way to POST a URL without having to know any programming knowledge. It simply acts as a frontend for the endpoint, with the backend for this page literally just being an AJAX post request.

To use it, you need to put a direct link to the URL of your image.

Though it shoudn't concern you all that much, you should know that if you are submitting a URL, I am logging your IP. I don't process the information or sell it off or anything shady like that, I just need to make sure that if anyone submits anything that conflicts with copyright, or the law, I have someone to report. But, in general, it shouldn't be something that concerns you. There's no way to get that information out of the database without having access to the actual database itself.

Have fun with your dogs!
